CWAIA Documents & Processes
New Members / Subscription to CWAIA:
  • Send any current TC officer an email with a brief introduction. In your joining email, please include your affiliation, email (institutional or ieee.org)(optional, if you opt in to join the mailing list) and link to your Google Scholar profile (optional).
  • Name, affiliation and Google Scholar picture will be displayed on TC website. Mailing list is only used for notices and dicussion related to TC activities. If you do not wish to appear on the TC website, please tell us in the joining email. If you opt in to join the mailing list by providing your email, you can unsubscribe at any time here.

Existing Members — periodically check:

  • Your info in the members catalog is up to date by informing us of any changes.
  • Open CFPs in the Events/CFPs section. If you wish to add any activities you are organising as IEEE IES events, send us an email so we can add it.
  • If you work with other TC members, and publish in an IEEE IES venue, you can choose to inform us of the details to have work added to the Joint Papers page.

Removal from CWAIA:

  • Send any current TC officer an email requesting to be removed. Your details will be removed from the members catalog and mailing list.
  • If you would like to unsubscribe from the mailing list, but remain a member, you can do so here.
